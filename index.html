Triathlon

The Triathlon will be held at Round Valley Reservoir in New Jersey. A minimum donation of $15 will be required to participate in the event (see registration section below). All participants will receive a participation ribbon. 1st-3rd place will get medals. Water stations will be set up along the route. Lifeguards on boats will be monitoring the swimmers, and will be launched from the boat drop area. We will have medical staff just in case. The State Park Police will be there in case anything happens. Most triathlons have people bring their own bikes, so we’re going to do that too. We will provide bike racks.

Our event merchandise includes t-shirts in a variety of colors, keychains, and an NKF stress ball.

Cook-Off

People can share their own healthy kidney friendly recipes on Instagram with the hashtag #kidneycookoff or with the registration section below. Using the link below people can vote on recipes, and the winners will be invited to compete in the cookoff. The top recipes will be placed into our cookbook, and the other ones will be discarded. The winners will compete in the cook off for a small cash prize and there will be 3 winners. The contest will only be open to people living in the United States of America. People from other countries can participate in the online recipe submission and voting. They will compete with each other and against celebrity chef Aaron McCargo Jr. Because some of the winners may not be able to afford to come, we will pay from their travel and stay expenses, and their ingredients. The food will be judged based on taste and presentation. Judges are Katie Lee Joel and local chef Joe Koeing.
